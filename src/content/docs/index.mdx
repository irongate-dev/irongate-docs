## What is Irongate?

Irongate is a self-hosted authentication and identity platform. Drop it into your
infrastructure, point your apps at it, and get production-grade auth without building it yourself.

## Features

- Email and password authentication
- OAuth2 social login (Google, GitHub, and more)
- Multi-factor authentication (TOTP, SMS, passkeys)
- Magic link authentication
- Organization management and multi-tenancy
- Session management and revocation
- Admin dashboard

## Self-hosted. Open source.

Irongate runs in your infrastructure. Your user data never leaves your control.
